The population size of Orange-crowned Warbler (<div>Paruline verd&amp;acirc;tre</div>) in the Ring of Fire region was 1.158 (0.903, 1.519) million individuals based on the [BAM National Models](https://dx.doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.4018335).
