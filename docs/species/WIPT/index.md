The population size of Willow Ptarmigan (<div>Lagop&amp;egrave;de des saules</div>) in the Ring of Fire region was 0.001 (0, 0.004) million individuals based on the [BAM National Models](https://dx.doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.4018335).
