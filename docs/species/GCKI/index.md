The population size of Golden-crowned Kinglet (Roitelet &amp;agrave; couronne dor&amp;eacute;e) in the Ring of Fire region was 9.741 (8.704, 10.837) million individuals based on the [BAM National Models](https://dx.doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.4018335).
